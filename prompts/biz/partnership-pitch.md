# Write a Partnership Pitch

**Use case:** Pitch a partnership, collaboration, or co-marketing arrangement to another company or creator.
**Works with:** Claude (any version)

---

Write a partnership pitch to [ORGANIZATION OR PERSON].

- What we bring: [YOUR VALUE — audience, assets, distribution, capabilities]
- What we want: [CO-MARKETING / REVENUE SHARE / DISTRIBUTION / CO-CREATION / ACCESS]
- Framing: lead with mutual benefit, state our ask second
- Format: [EMAIL / ONE-PAGER / DECK OUTLINE]
- Tone: peer-to-peer — we are bringing value, not asking for a favor

**Partnership pitch principle:**
Lead with what they get, not what you need. Position yourself as a peer with something valuable to offer, not as someone seeking a favor. The pitch should make them think "this benefits us" before it asks them for anything.
