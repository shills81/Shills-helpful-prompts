# Write a Follow-Up or Check-In Message

**Use case:** Write a short, value-adding follow-up for any professional context: sales, grants, partnerships, or client pipeline.
**Works with:** Claude (any version)

---

Write a follow-up message to [NAME / ROLE] about [TOPIC].

- Context: [LAST TOUCHPOINT AND WHEN IT WAS]
- Tone: [WARM / PROFESSIONAL / BRIEF]
- Goal: [GET A REPLY / CONFIRM NEXT STEP / SHARE AN UPDATE / REOPEN A STALLED CONVERSATION]
- Max 100 words
- No em dashes

**The value-add rule:**
The best follow-ups add something new — a relevant piece of information, a link, a development since the last conversation. "Just checking in" is the worst follow-up. Give them a reason to reply.
