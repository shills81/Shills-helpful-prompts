# Write a Cold Outreach Email

**Use case:** Write a short, targeted cold email for any outreach goal: intro meeting, partnership, grant inquiry, collaboration.
**Works with:** Claude (any version)

---

Write a cold outreach email to [ROLE / NAME] at [ORGANIZATION].

- Goal: [INTRO MEETING / PARTNERSHIP INQUIRY / COLLABORATION / GRANT INQUIRY]
- My context: [ONE SENTENCE — who you are and why it's relevant to them]
- Their context: [WHAT THEY DO AND WHY YOU'RE REACHING OUT TO THEM SPECIFICALLY]
- The ask: [SPECIFIC, YES/NO REQUEST — e.g. "Would you have 20 minutes to connect?" not "I'd love to explore opportunities"]
- Max 150 words
- No em dashes
- Output 3 subject line options

**Rules for cold email:**
- Lead with what is relevant to them, not your backstory
- The ask should be a yes/no question, not an open-ended invitation
- Short wins — 100 words beats 300 words for cold outreach
- One email, one ask — do not stack multiple requests
