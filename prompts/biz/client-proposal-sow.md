# Write a Client Proposal or Statement of Work

**Use case:** Write a professional proposal or SOW for a new client engagement — from a simple service agreement to a detailed project scope.
**Works with:** Claude (any version)

---

Write a client proposal for [CLIENT NAME OR TYPE].

- Service: [WHAT IS BEING DELIVERED]
- Duration: [TIMEFRAME]
- Deliverables: [LIST — specific and measurable]
- Investment: [PRICE / TIER]
- Payment terms: [UPFRONT / MILESTONE-BASED / MONTHLY / ANNUAL]

Include:
1. Executive summary (2–3 sentences)
2. Scope of work (what is included — and what is explicitly not included)
3. Timeline with milestones
4. Investment and payment terms
5. Next steps (what the client needs to do to proceed)

Tone: professional, confident.

**The out-of-scope section:**
Always include an explicit out-of-scope section. Vague SOWs create scope creep. The client should know exactly what they are and are not getting.
