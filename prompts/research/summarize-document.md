# Summarize a Long Document or Transcript

**Use case:** Extract the essential information from a long document, transcript, RFP, or article — with a focus on what is actionable or surprising.
**Works with:** Claude (any version)

---

Summarize this [DOCUMENT / TRANSCRIPT / ARTICLE / REPORT / RFP].

- Summary length: [3 BULLETS / 1 PARAGRAPH / 1 PAGE]
- Focus on: [KEY DECISIONS / ACTION ITEMS / STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS / DATA POINTS / REQUIREMENTS]
- Flag: anything that contradicts what I already believe or plan to do, and anything that requires immediate action

Here is the content: [PASTE]

**The contradiction flag:**
Asking Claude to surface contradictions is the most underused part of summarization. A neutral summary tells you what is there. The flag tells you what you might have missed or what challenges your assumptions. Always include it.

**For RFPs and grant documents specifically:**
Focus on: eligibility requirements (what disqualifies you), evaluation criteria (what they actually score on), word limits and format requirements, and submission deadline with timezone.
