# Define Your AI Agent's Category Bet

**Use case:** Identify the specific product category an AI agent could create — not just a feature, but a defensible market position.
**Works with:** Claude (any version)

---

Forget wrapper chatbots. If an AI chat agent embedded in [YOUR APP/PRODUCT] were worth $1B in five years, what category would it have created?

Give me 5 candidate category definitions, each with:
- The core insight
- The behavior it unlocks that wasn't possible before
- The incumbent product or workflow it displaces
- Why the window is open right now (timing, infrastructure, or behavior shift)

Rank them by defensibility.

Context:
- Product: [DESCRIBE YOUR APP — what it does, who uses it]
- Agent capabilities today: [WHAT THE AGENT CAN DO NOW]
- User base: [WHO YOUR USERS ARE]
